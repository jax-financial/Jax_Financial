Our Expertise

Personal Solutions

At Jax Capital Strategies, we offer individuals and families pioneering products and services designed to foster, safeguard, and preserve their wealth throughout life's various phases. Tailoring our approach to your unique needs, aspirations, and risk tolerance, we collaborate with you to illuminate your path forward and devise a roadmap for success. Explore some of our primary services outlined below.

Wealth Management and Retirement Planning

Partnering with our clients, we create and execute a wide array of strategies for wealth accumulation and retirement income. This includes providing insurance products and diverse investment programs, such as separately managed portfolios and mutual funds, as well as access to external wealth management services. Our steadfast process ensures that investment assets are managed in accordance with your risk tolerance and desired returns. We maintain ongoing vigilance over your plan, making adjustments and adaptations as your needs evolve over time.

Estate Planning

A meticulously crafted estate conservation strategy has the power to significantly reduce tax obligations and provide robust protection for your cherished ones. Collaborating closely with you and your team of advisors, we'll analyze the implications of both state and federal taxes on your estate. From there, we'll recommend tailored tactics aimed at mitigating tax burdens, all while aligning with your personal values and the needs of your family.

Asset Protection

An abundance of financial strategies and retirement income plans exist to aid in building assets for the future, protecting both personal and business assets from liabilities, and ensuring smooth asset transfer to future generations. Let us guide you in determining the optimal path forward tailored to your unique circumstances and objectives.

Charitable Planning

Engaging in charitable planning empowers you to champion the organizations and causes close to your heart, all while enjoying immediate income streams and minimizing tax liabilities. With a multitude of charitable giving strategies at your disposal, let us collaborate with you to craft and implement a charitable giving blueprint that harmonizes with your individual aspirations and philanthropic endeavors.

Investment Management

Effectively managing your wealth hinges on grasping your overarching investment goals. Our robust investment advisory services employ a structured approach that extends beyond conventional asset allocation. We delve into critical considerations like risk tolerance and investment timelines, ensuring a comprehensive strategy tailored to your needs.

Financial Planning

Tailoring our approach to each client's unique circumstances, including their income, risk tolerance, values, and family dynamics, we offer customized financial planning services. Our collaborative process involves identifying and prioritizing individual goals, exploring diverse options, devising effective strategies, implementing and executing the plan, and regularly evaluating its performance to make necessary adjustments.

Disability and Long Term Care

Crafting a robust retirement strategy necessitates safeguarding both assets and income. Developing a comprehensive plan that integrates long-term care and disability insurance can establish the essential equilibrium within a portfolio, fortifying stability and asset protection.

401(k) and IRA Rollovers

Upon departing a job or retiring, a pivotal choice arises regarding your 401(k) funds. Although retaining those assets within the former employer's plan remains viable, opting for a rollover merits serious consideration. Let us assist you in navigating this decision and determining the optimal path forward for your circumstances.

Business Solutions

At Jax Capital Strategies, we extend a comprehensive suite of solutions tailored to cater to the financial requirements of business owners across all phases of the business life cycle. By considering their individual values and financial aspirations, we craft and implement bespoke strategies aimed at assisting entrepreneurs in achieving their goals. Explore a selection of our prominent services outlined below.

Executive Benefits

The vitality of many businesses hinges on the talent, dedication, and work ethic of their key executives. Executive benefit packages serve as a vital tool in attracting, incentivizing, and retaining top-tier employees, thereby fostering the health and stability of your company. We offer expertise in non-qualified plans, supplemental employee retirement plans, split dollar plans, and an array of additional services to support your needs in this realm.

Succession Planning

Effective succession planning empowers owners to transition their business on their terms, readying the company for transfer to family members, key personnel, external entities, or charitable organizations. It's imperative that this process ensures personal financial stability, preserves harmony, and optimizes the business's value.

Key Person Insurance

Key person life insurance provides a safety net by offering a death benefit that compensates an employer for the loss of a vital asset: the key person. This coverage ensures business continuity for employees, customers, and creditors, shielding against disruptions in sales, momentum, and credit. Additionally, it can facilitate the recruitment and development of a successor for the key employee.

Buy-Sell Agreements

A buy-sell agreement serves as a legally binding contract among business co-owners, dictating the course of action in the event of a co-owner's departure, whether voluntary or involuntary. A funded cross-purchase buy-sell plan leverages life insurance to guarantee adequate funding, ensuring liquidity when the agreement is activated.

Deferred Compensation

Deferred compensation entails a formal agreement between an employer and an employee, wherein the employee elects to defer a portion of their compensation, allowing the company to invest it on their behalf until a predetermined future date when it is disbursed back to them. This arrangement serves as a versatile tool for attracting and motivating key employees.

财富管理和退休规划

与客户携手合作，我们制定并执行多种财富积累和退休收入的策略。这包括提供保险产品和多样化的投资计划， 如分别管理的投资组合和共同基金，以及获取外部财富管理服务的途径。我们坚定的流程确保投资资产根据您的风险承受能力和期望的回报进行管理。 我们持续监控您的计划，随着时间推移不断进行调整和适应，以满足您不断变化的需求。

财产规划

慈善规划

参与慈善规划使您能够支持您心仪的组织和事业，同时享受即时的收入流，并最小化税务责任。有着多种慈善捐赠策略可供选择，让我们与您合作制定并实施一份与您个人愿望和慈善事业相协调的慈善捐赠蓝图。

残疾和长期护理

制定强大的退休策略需要保护资产和收入。制定一个综合计划，整合长期护理和残疾保险，可以在投资组合中建立必要的平衡，巩固稳定性和资产保护。

401(k)和IRA转账

在离职或退休时，关于您的401(k)资金会出现一个关键选择。虽然保留这些资产在以前的雇主计划内仍然是可行的，选择进行转账值得认真考虑。让我们协助您在做出这个决定并确定适合您情况的最佳前进路径时进行导航。

企业解决方案

在Jax Capital Strategies，我们提供一系列全面的解决方案，旨在满足企业所有阶段的业主的财务需求。通过考虑他们个人的价值观和财务愿望，我们制定并实施定制的策略，旨在帮助企业家实现他们的目标。以下是我们一些重要服务的选择。

高管福利

许多企业的活力取决于其关键高管的才华、奉献精神和职业道德。高管福利计划是吸引、激励和留住顶级员工的重要工具，从而促进公司的健康和稳定。我们在非合格计划、补充员工退休计划、分期付款计划以及其他一系列服务方面提供专业知识，以支持您在这个领域的需求。

继任规划

有效的继任规划使业主能够按照自己的条件进行业务过渡，使公司为转让给家庭成员、关键人员、外部实体或慈善组织做好准备。这个过程必须确保个人财务稳定，保持和谐，并优化企业的价值。

关键人员保险

关键人寿险通过提供一项死亡福利来为雇主提供保障，以补偿关键人员的丧失。这种保险确保了员工、客户和债权人的业务连续性，防范了销售、势头和信用中的中断。此外，它还可以促进对关键员工的继任者的招聘和发展。

买卖协议

买卖协议是业务合伙人之间的具有法律约束力的合同，规定了在合伙人离开的情况下采取的行动，无论是自愿的还是非自愿的。经过资金支持的交叉购买买卖计划利用寿险来保证充足的资金，确保在激活协议时具备流动性。

延期薪酬

延期薪酬涉及雇主与雇员之间的正式协议，雇员选择推迟他们薪酬的一部分，使公司可以代表他们进行投资，直到预定的未来日期，然后将其返还给他们。这种安排是吸引和激励关键员工的一种多功能工具。
