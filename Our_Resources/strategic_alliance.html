Strategic Alliances

Eagle Strategies, LLC

Eagle Strategies offers fee-based financial planning, investment advisory services, and access to investment management programs. As an "Investment Adviser" registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Eagle Strategies LLC operates as an indirect, fully owned subsidiary of New York Life Insurance Company.

Ash Brokerage

Ash Brokerage stands as an independent, family-operated insurance brokerage general agency. Offering an extensive array of products and support for life insurance, long-term care, disability income, and annuities, Ash fills a vital role not covered by New York Life or its subsidiaries. Our collaboration with Ash aligns seamlessly with our mission to furnish clients with impartial solutions tailored to their unique requirements.

New York Life

As financial professionals, we take pride in our affiliation with New York Life Insurance Company and its wholly owned subsidiaries. New York Life offers a range of products, including life insurance, fixed annuities, and long-term care insurance. As a mutual insurance company, New York Life ranks among the largest in the United States and boasts the highest financial strength ratings from all four major credit rating agencies: A.M. Best (A++), Fitch (AAA), Moody's Investor Service (Aaa), and Standard & Poor's (AA+). (Source: Third-Party Ratings Reports as of 9/30/201.)

战略联盟

鹰策略有限责任公司

鹰策略提供基于费用的财务规划、投资咨询服务以及投资管理计划的访问。作为一家在美国证券交易委员会注册的“投资顾问”，鹰策略有限责任公司是纽约人寿保险公司的间接全资子公司。

艾仕经纪

Ash Brokerage是一家独立的家族经营的保险经纪总代理。提供广泛的寿险、长期护理、残疾收入和年金产品以及支持，Ash在纽约人寿及其子公司未涵盖的领域发挥着重要作用。我们与Ash的合作与我们的使命完美契合，为客户提供根据其独特需求量身定制的公正解决方案。

纽约人寿

作为金融专业人士，我们为与纽约人寿保险公司及其全资子公司的关联感到自豪。纽约人寿提供一系列产品，包括寿险、固定年金和长期护理保险。作为一家相互保险公司，纽约人寿在美国排名最大，并在所有四家主要信用评级机构中获得最高的财务实力评级：A.M. Best（A++）、Fitch（AAA）、Moody's Investor Service（Aaa）和标准普尔（AA+）。 （资料来源：截至201年9月30日的第三方评级报告。）
