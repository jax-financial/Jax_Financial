Jing Xu

Xu Jing is a dynamic force within the insurance market, boasting over five years of licensed professional experience. Specializing in Annuities, Long Term Care, and Life Insurance, she is dedicated to crafting comprehensive financial solutions tailored to each client's unique needs. Her stellar performance in the industry is underscored by accolades such as the Rookie Agent of the Year award for the New York Life Long Island General Office in 2018 and membership in the prestigious Court of the Million Dollar Round Table.

Xu Jing's proactive approach and unwavering commitment to providing exceptional service reflect her pursuit of excellence and client satisfaction. With a sharp eye for detail and a genuine passion for helping others secure their financial futures, Xu Jing serves as a trusted advisor and ally for those navigating the intricacies of insurance and financial planning. Beyond her professional endeavors, Xu Jing remains actively engaged in her community, participating in humanitarian initiatives aimed at improving the lives of others. With her impressive track record, deep understanding of the insurance landscape, and unwavering dedication to her clients' success, Xu Jing is poised to make a lasting impact in the insurance sector for years to come.

徐靜

徐靜是保險市場中的一股動力，擁有超過五年的專業許可經驗。她專攻年金、長期護理和人壽保險，致力於為每位客戶量身定制全面的財務解決方案。她在行業中的出色表現得到了肯定，如2018年紐約人壽長島總辦事處新秀獎以及成為百萬圓桌會員等。

徐靜的積極主動和對提供優質服務的堅定承諾，體現了她對卓越和客戶滿意度的追求。憑藉對細節的敏銳觸覺和對幫助他人確保財務未來的真正熱情，徐靜成為了那些在保險和財務規劃的複雜領域中航行的人的值得信賴的顧問和盟友。除了職業努力外，徐靜還積極參與社區活動，參與旨在改善他人生活的人道主義倡議。憑藉著令人印象深刻的履歷、對保險行業的深刻理解和對客戶成功的堅定承諾，徐靜將在未來多年中對保險行業產生持久影響。
